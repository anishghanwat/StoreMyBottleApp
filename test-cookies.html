🍪 HttpOnly Cookie Test

Testing secure session management with HttpOnly cookies

Test 1: Login and Set Cookies

Login to set HttpOnly cookies. Check browser DevTools → Application → Cookies.

Test 2: Check Cookies

Verify cookies are set. Note: HttpOnly cookies cannot be read by JavaScript!

Test 3: Authenticated Request

Make an authenticated request using cookies (no Authorization header needed).

Test 4: Logout and Clear Cookies

Logout to clear cookies. Verify cookies are removed in DevTools.

📋 Instructions

  1. Open Browser DevTools (F12)
  2. Go to Application tab → Cookies → http://localhost:8000
  3. Click Login button above
  4. Check DevTools - you should see access_token and refresh_token cookies
  5. Verify cookies have HttpOnly flag
  6. Click Get Profile to test authenticated request
  7. Click Logout and verify cookies are cleared
